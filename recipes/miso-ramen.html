Miso Ramen

Description

Miso ramen is a hearty and comforting ramen with a broth flavored with fermented soybean paste. Originating from Hokkaido, it’s perfect for colder days.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Ramen noodles
  • Corn kernels
  • Butter
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pot and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  3. Stir in miso paste and soy sauce until dissolved.
  4. Cook ramen noodles as per package instructions.
  5. Serve noodles in the broth, topped with corn, butter, and green onions.
