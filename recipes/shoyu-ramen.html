Shoyu Ramen

Description

Shoyu ramen features a soy sauce-based broth that delivers a flavorful and savory experience. Itâ€™s one of the most classic types of ramen found in Japan.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup dashi stock
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Ramen noodles
  • Chashu pork slices
  • Menma (bamboo shoots)
  • Soft boiled eggs
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth and dashi in a pot and bring to a simmer.
  2. Add soy sauce, mirin, and sesame oil. Stir well.
  3. Cook ramen noodles as directed on the package.
  4. Place noodles in a bowl, pour over the hot broth.
  5. Top with chashu pork, menma, eggs, and green onions.
